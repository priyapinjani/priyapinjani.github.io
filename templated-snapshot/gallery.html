Curator

Priya Pinjani is an architect from Karachi, Pakistan. A graduate of Indus Valley School of Art and Architecture, she practiced hospitality and residential design for a year and a half before making her way to New York and The New School. While formal design is something she enjoys thoroughly, she has always been interested in politics of space, right to the city and the social ecosystems that operate within an urban environment. She tries to synthesize these interests with Design and Urban Ecologies at Parsons, and hopes to go back home with a much broader, and relevant, definition of both architecture and design.
